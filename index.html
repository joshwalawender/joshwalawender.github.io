About Me

I'm an astronomer living on the Big Island of Hawaii. My research involves studying how young stars interact with their parent molecular cloud. I'm also an alumnus of The University of California at Berkeley where I majored in physics and astrophysics. I went on to earn my PhD in Astrophysical and Planetary Sciences at the University of Colorado at Boulder.
